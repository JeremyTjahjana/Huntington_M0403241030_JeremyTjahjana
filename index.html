Projects

Right Over

Right Over was build as a project for the Apple Swift Student Challenge 2024. Right over is a mobile application designed to solve the common problem of food waste by transforming kitchen leftovers into delicious and creative meals. It empowers users to maximize available ingredients by allowing them to select up to three key items, after which the app generates one of 30 predefined recipes that are suited for that combination. The app also features a unique pantry menu that provides engaging, video game-like descriptions of a food item's nutritional value, helping users learn about the ingredients they have on hand in a fun and memorable way.
